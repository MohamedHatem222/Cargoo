Introduction

Welcome to Cargo Travels LLC. At Cargo Travels LLC, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or otherwise interact with us. By accessing or using our website or services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website or services.

Information We Collect :

Personal Information

We may collect various types of personal information, including but not limited to:
1. Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
2. Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and payment history when you make a purchase or booking through our website.
3. Booking and Travel Information: Information related to your travel bookings, such as flight details, hotel reservations, and travel preferences.
4. Account Information: Username and password if you create an account on our website.
5.Communication Data: Records of your interactions with us, including emails, chat logs, and customer support inquiries.

Non-Personal Information

We may share your personal information with:
1. Service Providers: Third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as airlines, hotels, and payment processors.
2. Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

How We Use Your Information

1. We use your personal information for various purposes, including:
  1. Providing Services: To fulfill your travel bookings, process payments, and provide customer support.
  2. Improving Services: To enhance and personalize your experience on our website, develop new features, and conduct research and analysis.
  3. Marketing and Communication: To send you promotional materials, updates, and information about our services, with your consent.

Information Sharing

1. We may share your personal information with:
  1. Service Providers: Third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as airlines, hotels, and payment processors.
  2. Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.
